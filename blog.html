Refund & Cancellation Policies

Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you’re not happy, follow the steps below:

  • 30‑day return window. You have 30 calendar days from the date you receive your order to request a refund.
  • Item condition. The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and returned with all accessories and documentation.
  • Return authorization. Contact our support team at info@sparepartshub.co.za to obtain a Return Authorization Number (RAN).
  • Shipping costs. Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer, unless the item is defective or we made an error.
  • Refund processing. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we’ll issue a refund to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • Non‑refundable items. Custom or special‑order parts are non‑refundable unless they are defective.

Cancellation Policy

We process cancellations as quickly as possible. Please note the following:

  • Before dispatch. If your order has not yet been shipped, you can cancel it free of charge. Email us with your order number and we’ll stop the shipment immediately.
  • After dispatch. Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled. You’ll need to follow the refund procedure above if you wish to return the item.
  • Partial cancellations. If you want to remove only part of an order, let us know before the order ships and we’ll adjust the invoice accordingly.
